In this section, you will find straightforward and understandable system requirements that will help ensure the reliable operation of the Platform and the Registry instance.

*_System requirements for the Platform instance_* detail the technical specifications for deploying your Platform in various environments, mainly _AWS_ and _vSphere_, and provide an overview of the infrastructure service costs.

*_System requirements for the Registry instance_* provide specific characteristics and needs for deploying the registry on the _Platform_. This section also contains exhaustive information about the registry cost.
